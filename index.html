About

EmmsTechnonlogy (EmmsTech) is a nonprofit movement and an educational events that highlight, educate, and celebrate women of color (WOC) and the underrepresented in technology, innovation and entrepreneurialism. Our events and workshops offer a variety of speakers, coding classes, and workshops, empowering women of color, girls, and women, the underrepresented and diverse communities. We seek to create a shifting in diversity and inclusion within technology by offering content and impactful events.

Our aim is to give WOC and the underrepresented the opportunity to explore and learn about tech, and to inspire to pursue it, to create a community where they can spread their knowledge.

Inclusivity and accessibility is our ultimate goal and we intend to offer innovative engagement opportunities to as many diverse audiences as possible. We seek to create a shift in diversity and inclusion within technology by offering content and impactful events.

We love learning new things as much we love to share that knowledge. Become partner, sponsor that we can create represented and diversity technology.
