About

Emms are founded by 4 women that want to help out girls and minority. Our aim is to give the opportunity to explore and learn about tech, and to inspire them to pursue it, to create a community where they can spread their knowledge.

We would like to believe our target group are age 16+.

We are non-profit movement. We do it for the greater cause!
